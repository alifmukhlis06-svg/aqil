Understanding Domestic Violence

Learn about the causes, effects, and types of domestic violence in Malaysia

What is Domestic Violence?

Domestic violence is a pattern of abusive behavior used to control a partner or family member. It can take many forms and affects people of all backgrounds.

Types of Domestic Violence:

  • Physical: Hitting, slapping, pushing, restraining
  • Emotional: Verbal abuse, intimidation, isolation
  • Financial: Controlling money, preventing employment
  • Sexual: Forced sexual acts, reproductive coercion
  • Digital: Online harassment, monitoring devices

Causes of Domestic Violence

Understanding the root causes helps in prevention and intervention:

  • Learned behavior from family or society
  • Substance abuse issues
  • Unresolved trauma or mental health issues
  • Cultural and societal norms
  • Economic stress and unemployment
  • Power and control issues
