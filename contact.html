Support Services

Immediate Safety Planning

If you're in danger, we can help you create a personalized safety plan. This includes:

  • Identifying safe places in your home
  • Creating a code word with trusted friends/family
  • Preparing an emergency bag with essentials
  • Documenting incidents safely

Legal Assistance

Free legal advice and support available:

  • Protection Orders (IPO/EPO)
  • Divorce proceedings
  • Child custody matters
  • Police report assistance

Safety First - Important Tips

If you're in immediate danger:

  1. Call 999 immediately
  2. Try to get to a room with a lock and a phone
  3. If you can leave safely, go to a trusted neighbor's house or public place
  4. Don't worry about belongings - your safety comes first

If using a shared computer/phone:

  • Clear your browser history after visiting support sites
  • Use private/incognito browsing mode
  • Create a new email account for support communications
  • Save important numbers under different names

Remember: You have the right to live free from violence and fear.
